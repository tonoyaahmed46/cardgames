Rummy

Rummy is a game played with two decks, as players pick and disregard cards from the two piles to score points.



How to play Rummy.





Blackjack

Blackjack is a commonly played casino game where players compete against the dealer, not the other players.



How to play Blackjack.





Spades

Spades is a trick-taking card game where the objective is to take at least the number of tricks that were bid in the hand before.



How to play Spades.





Cheat or BS

Cheat is game where players aim to get rid of their cards, using deception to do so.



How to play Cheat





Go Fish

In Go Fish, each player asks other players for cards and are told to go fish from central pile if denied.



How to play Go Fish.





Spit or Speed

Speed is a game for only two players in which each player tries to get rid of their cards by placing them in the center pile in order of hierarchy.



How to play Spit.





Crazy 8s

Crazy 8s is a shedding type card game where the objective is to get rid of the entire pile by suit or number.



How to play Crazy 8s.





War

War is played with cards initially placed face down, with all players piling cards into the center, attempting to rid their pile.



How to play War.





Spoons

Spoons is a collecting card game where the cards circulate in a circle for one player to gather one type of card and players grab spoons from the center to ensure their place in the next round.



How to play Spoons.





Slapjack

In slapjack, the entire pile is collected each time a player slaps a jack, with the objective being to gain the entire deck.



How to play Slapjack.
